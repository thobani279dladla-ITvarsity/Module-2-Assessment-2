THE TOMORROW

Released on Amazon Prime early in July 2021, The Tomorrow War is an indulgent nod to classic Sci-Fi. This well-executed time travel meets alien invasion flick, starring Chris Pratt, features uniquely strange aliens. There is no hint of what the creatures look like before Pratt's character, Dan Forester, is sent to the future.

Released Date: July 2021

Starring: Chris Pratt

Genres: Science Fiction
